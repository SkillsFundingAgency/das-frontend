WHERE ARE YOU BASED?

Whether you’re based in Scotland, Northern Ireland or Wales, there’s plenty of information for you to delve into.

If you’re a budding apprentice, you’ll discover a range of local apprenticeships to help you build the experience and skills employers are looking for - no matter what stage you’re at.

For employers, there’s plenty of information about the benefits of hiring an apprentice and how you can get involved. Choose from the options below to get started.

Scotland

No matter what stage you're at, Scottish Apprenticeships help you get set for a bright future. And now, there are more ways than ever to get involved:

  • Foundation Apprenticeships to kick start your career while you’re still at school
  • Modern Apprenticeships to work, learn and earn while you get the skills you need
  • Graduate apprenticeships to get a job, get paid and get a degree at the same time

Explore apprenticeships in Scotland

Northern Ireland

In Northern Ireland, apprenticeships are looked after by the Department for the Economy. They provide apprentices with the training and qualifications they need to get ahead in their chosen career. There's a great range available:

  • Level 2 and Level 3 Apprenticeships to work towards an industry-approved apprenticeship framework, while in paid employment.
  • Higher Level Apprenticeships to gain a qualification from Level 4 to Level 7, while working with an employer to gain on-the-job experience.

Explore apprenticeships in Northern Ireland

Wales

If you are over 16, living in Wales and not in full-time education you can apply for an apprenticeship. These include:

  • Foundation Apprenticeship
  • Apprenticeship
  • Higher Apprenticeship
