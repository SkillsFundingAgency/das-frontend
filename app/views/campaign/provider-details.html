ABOUT THE APPRENTICESHIP

The Business Administrator Level 3 Apprenticeship Standard is designed to develop your skills and knowledge further and enhance your career prospects in the business world. As a natural progression from the Level 2 Intermediate Business Administration Apprenticeship, this Level 3 Apprenticeship is suitable for anyone working in either of the following roles:

  • Administration Assistant
  • Personal Assistant
  • Business Administrator
  • Office Assistant
  • Office Supervisor

PROVIDER INFORMATION

Paragon - Skills for the industry is a leading Grade 2, Good, Ofsted rated national training provider delivering first-class apprenticeship programmes across a variety of sectors including; Adult Care, Childcare & Education, Hair & Beauty, Customer Experience, Automotive & Marine, Leadership & Management and Accountancy.

We deliver high-quality apprenticeship programmes and employers will receive customised programmes that fit their unique business to drive their strategy, whilst learning plans are tailored to each individual apprentice's needs. All programmes contain the essential elements expected from an apprenticeship and will lead to an industry-wide recognised qualification.
