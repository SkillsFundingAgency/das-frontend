Cookies preferences

We use 3 types of cookie. You can choose which cookies you're happy for us to use.

Analytics

We use Analytics to measure how you use the website so we can improve it based on user needs.

Cookies may be set by other third-party websites to do things like measure how you view YouTube videos that are on the website.

Marketing

These cookies help us improve the relevancy of advertising campaigns you receive from us.

Strictly necessary cookies

These essential cookies do things like:

  • remember the notifications you've seen so we do not show them to you again
  • remember your progress through a form

They always need to be on.
